About the Author

Six months of the year Steve lives an off the grid lifestyle while hosting hikers from the Appalachian Trail in the mountains of western Maine. The remaining six months, he visits loved ones in India as well as helping families there in need of various types of assistance. In between all of his trips he continues to walks in gratitude.
