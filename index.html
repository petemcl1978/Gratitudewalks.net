About the Author

For six months each year, Steve lives a simple off-the-grid life in the mountains of Western Maine. He hosts hikers walking the sacred path called the Appalachian trail. The other six months Steve visits loved ones in India. He has small projects there helping children get to school and sponsoring young women with sewing machines to support their own families.
